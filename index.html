Our Mission

At NP Racing, we are dedicated to pushing the limits of what's possible in the world of F1 in Schools. Our passion for motorsport drives our innovation and creativity, as we strive to build not just the fastest car, but the most efficient and technically advanced design.

Our goal is to not only compete but to learn valuable engineering, marketing, and teamwork skills that will prepare us for careers in the automotive and engineering industries.

About Us

NP Racing is a passionate group of students competing in the global F1 in Schools competition. We aim to design, manufacture, and race the fastest and most innovative model car, while gaining practical experience in project management, design, and engineering.

With a focus on sustainability and cutting-edge technology, we are pioneering new approaches to car design, aerodynamics, and efficiency, ensuring that we not only meet but exceed the expectations of the competition.
